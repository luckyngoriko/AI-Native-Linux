### Internet Society Foundation - Common Good Cyber Fund

- Status: Candidate
- Priority: High
- Application window: 2026-06-23 to 2026-08-04

## Structural Funding Work

### Legal / Fiscal Structure

- Status: Needed before some larger grants
- Reason: Common Good Cyber Fund and similar funders often require nonprofit organization status, equivalent legal entity, or fiscal host.
- Options:
  - create a Bulgarian nonprofit association/foundation
  - use an existing fiscal host
  - partner with an eligible nonprofit
  - keep individual applications for programs that allow individuals

### Public Project Maturity

- Status: Ongoing
- Needed for later funders:
  - public releases
  - clear roadmap
  - issues and contribution process
  - working prototype
  - security policy
  - evidence of adoption or review
  - public documentation
  - reproducible demos
  - stable license and governance
